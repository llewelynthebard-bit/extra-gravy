About

Extra Gravy is a four-piece bluegrass band based in Boulder, CO. With influences spanning classic bluegrass to jammin’ newgrass, Extra Gravy has a wide catalogue of new original music that blends bluegrass with other genres for creative and inspired songwriting. From high energy rippers to introspective tear-jerkers, Extra Gravy is rich in instrumentalism and lyricism. The members strive to bring forth creativity and emotion in their music while retaining the bluegrass roots they hold dear.
