About

Extra Gravy is a bluegrass band serving up heaping portions of old-time music with a generous side of soul. We play the kind of music that makes you want to stomp your feet and hug a stranger.

Started around a kitchen table and a six-pack, we've been picking, grinning, and occasionally arguing about tuning ever since. We've played barns, festivals, back porches, and at least one wedding that got a little out of hand.

When we're not on stage, we're probably cooking.
