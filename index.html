About

Extra Gravy is a finger-licking good four-piece bluegrass band based in Boulder, CO. With a colorful catalogue of original music, Extra Gravy blends traditional bluegrass with cross-genre influences for exciting and inspired songwriting. High energy rippers meet introspective tear-jerkers for an unforgettable set. Rich in instrumentalism and lyricism, Extra Gravy cooks up something newgrass while retaining all the soulful flavor that mountain music is all about.

Jonah Salzman-Cohen — Banjo, Vocals

Matthew Haithcock — Mandolin, Vocals

Jay Pepitone — Bass, Vocals

James Messner — Guitar, Vocals
